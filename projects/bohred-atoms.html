Bohred Atoms

These atoms are so bohred they can’t take it anymore!

Bohred Atoms is a local multiplayer 2D shooter game where each player shoots the other atom’s electrons with their nuclear bullets to destroy them.

It can be played online or downloaded to Windows here.

  • Engine: Unity
  • Language: C#
  • Platforms: Windows PC and Web
  • Published: Feb 2017
