About

On a typical day, you can find me either making games, playing games or thinking about stargazing... while Edinburgh rains. Apart from that, I also like playing around with technologies new to me. (Front-end web dev is the latest kick, the result is this website)

I'm originally from Taiwan but I lived in The Netherlands for a while. My interest in Software Engineering came about when I tried making a live wallpaper for my secondary school project. Well... the technical aspect of it didn't go very well but the learning experience made me realize how much I enjoyed software development.

Gaming has been a large part of my life and shaped much of who I am. So with the skills I'll acquire at university and on the side, I hope to create materials people can make fond memories with.
